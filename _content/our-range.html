* Great Taste Awards

Many Consumers will now recognize the Great Taste Awards logo that appears on food products - from chocolate to cheese and from sausage to soup. The Great Taste Awards have been described as the "the Oscar of the food world". Devised by The Guild of Fine Food and involving rigorous blind-tasting by teams of experts, on the the very best food products receive the covered Great Taste winners' stamp.

For more information: greattasteawards.co.uk
